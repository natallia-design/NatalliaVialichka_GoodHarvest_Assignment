Now in Season:

Avocado

  • Avocados contain naturally good fats
  • Avocados are a plant food and therefore the fat they contain is considered an “oil” and not a “solid fat”
  • Avocados are a great substitute for saturated or trans fats in your favorite dishes
  • Avocados are cholesterol and sodium free

How to Choose:

Look for firm avocados with a slight give when squeezed. Hard avocados will ripen in 3-5 days.

Tastes Like:

Creamy and mellow, with a buttery texture.

Best Eaten:

Raw

Nutrition:

  • An excellent source of healthy fats
  • A good source of vitamins B, E, and C, copper, and fiber
  • Contains more potassium than a banana.

In Season:

May - August
